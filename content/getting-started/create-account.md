# Create Your Authflow Account

Getting started with Authflow takes just a few minutes. Follow these steps to create your account.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A custom GPT created on ChatGPT (or plan to create one)
- An email address for registration
- A Stripe account (Authflow will guide you through this if you don't have one)

## Step-by-Step Registration

1. **Visit Authflow**: Go to [authflow.ai](https://authflow.ai?utm_source=github&utm_medium=docs&utm_campaign=github_docs&utm_content=createaccount_visit_cta)

2. **Click Get Started**: Click the **"Get Started"** or **"Start Monetizing for Free"** button

3. **Sign Up**: Enter your email address to create your account

4. **Verify Email**: Check your inbox and verify your email if required

5. **Access Dashboard**: You'll be taken to the Authflow dashboard at [app.authflow.ai](https://authflow.ai/start?utm_source=github&utm_medium=docs&utm_campaign=github_docs&utm_content=createaccount_dashboard_cta)

## What's Next?

After creating your account, you'll see the **Getting Started** checklist. This guides you through the essential setup steps to start monetizing your GPTs.

The checklist includes:
- Welcome Survey
- Accept Terms of Service
- Complete Your Profile
- Connect Stripe for Payouts
- Review Tax Readiness
- Review Stripe Settings
- Create Your First Paywall
- Welcome Your First Subscriber

Continue to the next guide to learn how to complete the onboarding process.
