The Authflow dashboard is your command center for managing paywalls, tracking revenue, and growing your GPT business. Here's what each section does and when to use it.

## Overview Section üìä

### Dashboard

Your main overview page showing key metrics at a glance.

**What you'll see:**
- Total revenue and earnings
- Number of active subscriptions
- Recent payments and transactions
- Quick stats (last 30 days, all time, etc.)
- List of all your paywalls with key metrics

**Use it for:** Getting a quick snapshot of your business performance

### Analytics

Detailed analytics and reporting for deep insights.

**What you'll see:**
- Revenue trends over time
- Subscription growth charts
- User engagement metrics
- Conversion rates
- Payment success/failure rates
- Date range filters (Today, Last 7 Days, Last 30 Days, etc.)

**Use it for:** Deep dive into your business performance and identifying trends

## Paywalls Section üîí

### Create Paywall

Build a new paywall for your GPT.

**What you'll see:**
- Form to configure paywall details
- Pricing options (one-time or subscription)
- Free trial settings
- Integration credentials (after creation)

**Use it for:** Creating new monetization options for your GPTs

### My Paywalls

Manage all your existing paywalls.

**What you'll see:**
- Table listing all paywalls
- Columns: Manage, Paywall Details, Pricing, Created Date, Statistics
- Search and filter options
- "Go" button to access each paywall's details
- Download authentication reports
- Metrics: Total Payout, Payments, Unique Auths, Paid Users

**Use it for:** Viewing all your paywalls, accessing integration details, monitoring performance, downloading reports

## Account Section üë§

### Plans & Billing

Manage your Authflow subscription plan.

**What you'll see:**
- Your current plan details
- Pricing tiers and features
- Billing information
- Payment method
- Usage limits (e.g., "13 out of 1000000 paywalls")

**Use it for:** Upgrading/downgrading your plan, viewing billing history

### My Account

Manage your personal account settings.

**What you'll see:**
- Profile information
- Email settings
- Password changes
- Account preferences

**Use it for:** Updating your account information

### Stripe Connect

Manage your Stripe account connection.

**What you'll see:**
- Stripe account status
- Connection status
- Ability to reset/reconnect Stripe
- Revenue events and payout information
- Stripe dashboard link

**Use it for:** Verifying Stripe connection, resetting if needed, viewing payout information

## Quick Navigation Tips

- **Left Sidebar**: Always visible for quick navigation between sections
- **Breadcrumbs**: At the top show where you are (e.g., "Home / My Paywalls / Create Paywall")
- **Search**: Available in some sections (like My Paywalls) to quickly find specific paywalls
- **Filters**: Use date ranges and filters to analyze specific time periods
- **Action Buttons**: Look for "Create Paywall", "Go", "Download", etc. to perform actions
