# Complete the Onboarding Checklist

When you first log in to Authflow, you'll see a **Getting Started** checklist. This guides you through the essential setup steps. Here's what each step means and how to complete it.

## The Getting Started Checklist

### 1. Welcome Survey ✅

- **Purpose**: Help Authflow personalize your experience
- **Action**: Click **"Start"** and answer questions about your goals
- **Why**: This helps Authflow provide better recommendations and support

### 2. Accept Terms of Service ✅

- **Purpose**: Agree to Authflow's terms to unlock full platform capabilities
- **Action**: Click **"Review"** and read/accept the terms
- **Why**: Required to use the platform legally

### 3. Complete Your Profile ✅

- **Purpose**: Add personal or business details for identity verification and payouts
- **Action**: Click **"Review"** and fill in your information
- **Why**: Needed for tax purposes and to receive payments

### 4. Connect Stripe for Payouts ✅

- **Purpose**: Link your Stripe account to begin collecting payments
- **Action**: Click **"Review"** and follow the Stripe connection process
- **Why**: This is how you'll receive money from subscribers
- **Note**: If you don't have a Stripe account, you'll be guided to create one

### 5. Review Tax Readiness ✅

- **Purpose**: Ensure your Stripe Tax settings are configured
- **Action**: Click **"View"** and acknowledge your tax settings
- **Why**: Required for compliance and proper tax handling

### 6. Review Stripe Settings ✅

- **Purpose**: Follow recommended Stripe configurations to reduce friction and errors
- **Action**: Click **"View"** and review/update your Stripe settings
- **Why**: Proper settings ensure smooth payment processing and fewer failed transactions

### 7. Create Your First Paywall ✅

- **Purpose**: Restrict access to your GPT and start charging users instantly
- **Action**: Click **"Review"** to go to the paywall creation page
- **Why**: This is where you'll create the paywall that protects your GPT
- **Note**: See the [Create Your First Paywall](https://github.com/Snyder-Technology-LLC/authflow-guides-resources-docs/blob/main/content/paywalls/create-paywall.md) guide for detailed instructions

### 8. Welcome Your First Subscriber ⏳

- **Purpose**: Share your paywall link and start building your audience
- **Action**: Once you've created a paywall, share it and get your first subscriber
- **Why**: This completes your setup and gets you earning!

## Tracking Your Progress

You can see your completion percentage (e.g., "88% Complete") at the top of the checklist. You can also hide completed steps to focus on what's left.

## Tips for Success

- **Complete all steps**: Each step is important for a smooth monetization experience
- **Don't skip Stripe setup**: Proper Stripe configuration prevents payment issues later
- **Take your time with tax settings**: This ensures compliance and avoids problems
