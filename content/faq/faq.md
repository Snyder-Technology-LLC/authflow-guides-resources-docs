# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is Authflow?

Authflow is a platform that allows you to add secure paywalls to your custom GPTs on ChatGPT, enabling you to monetize them with subscriptions or one-time payments.

### How does Authflow work?

1. Sign up for Authflow
2. Connect your Stripe account
3. Create a paywall for your GPT
4. Integrate the paywall code into your GPT
5. Start earning from subscriptions/payments

### What do I need to get started?

- A custom GPT on ChatGPT
- An Authflow account
- A Stripe account (for payment processing)

### Is coding experience required?

No! Authflow is designed to be no-code. You can set up paywalls in minutes with copy/paste configuration only.

## Pricing & Payments

### How much does Authflow cost?

See the [Pricing Plans](/paywalls/authflow-pricing-plans) page for current pricing. Authflow offers a free tier to get started.

### What payment methods do you support?

Authflow supports all payment methods available through Stripe:
- Credit and debit cards
- Digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay)
- Bank transfers (in supported regions)
- And more

### How do I get paid?

Payments go directly to your connected Stripe account. Stripe handles payouts according to your account settings (typically weekly or monthly).

### What are the transaction fees?

All plans (except Enterprise with custom fees) charge a transaction fee per payment, plus standard Stripe processing fees. See [Pricing Plans](/paywalls/authflow-pricing-plans) for details.

## Features & Capabilities

### Can I create multiple paywalls?

Yes! You can create different paywalls for different GPTs or different subscription tiers (depending on your plan).

### Can I offer free trials or discounts?

Yes! Authflow supports free trials (1-14 days) and promotional pricing.

### What types of GPTs can I monetize?

You can monetize any custom GPT you've created on ChatGPT:
- Productivity tools
- Educational GPTs
- Creative assistants
- Business tools
- Specialized knowledge GPTs

### Can I use one paywall across multiple GPTs?

Yes! Copy the same schema into multiple GPTs. Users authenticate once and can access all GPTs sharing that paywall.

## Security & Compliance

### Is it secure?

Yes! Authflow uses Stripe for all payment processing, which is PCI DSS Level 1 certified and used by millions of businesses worldwide. Your payment data is never stored on Authflow's servers.

### Is Authflow compliant with global regulations?

Yes, Authflow uses Stripe, which is compliant with global payment regulations including GDPR, PCI DSS, and regional requirements.

## Technical Questions

### How does Authflow handle subscription renewals?

Authflow automatically handles subscription renewals. Users are charged automatically, and you'll receive notifications about renewals and any payment issues.

### Can I scale with Authflow?

Absolutely! Authflow is built to scale with your business. Whether you have 10 subscribers or 10,000, the platform handles it seamlessly.

### What about the GPT instructions character limit?

The Authflow instructions take up space in your GPT's 8,000 character limit. See [Character Limit Troubleshooting](/troubleshooting/character-limit) for optimization tips.
