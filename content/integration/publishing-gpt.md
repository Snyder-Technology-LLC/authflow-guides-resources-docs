## Publishing Options

### Option 1: Share via Link

Best for: Direct sharing, email lists, social media

1. Click **"Save"** in your GPT configuration
2. Click **"Share"** or **"Copy Link"**
3. Share the link with your audience

**Pros:**
- Immediate access
- Easy to track specific campaigns
- Can share before GPT Store approval

### Option 2: Publish to ChatGPT Store

Best for: Discovery, reaching new users

1. In your GPT settings, set visibility to **"Public"**
2. Click **"Publish"**
3. Your GPT enters the review queue
4. Once approved, it appears in the ChatGPT Store

**Pros:**
- Organic discovery
- Credibility from store presence
- Access to ChatGPT's user base

**Timeline:**
- Review typically takes 1-3 days
- May require adjustments if rejected

## Marketing Your GPT

### Build Your Audience

1. **Email List**
   - Collect emails through a landing page
   - Send launch announcement
   - Share updates and tips

2. **Social Media**
   - Twitter/X for AI community
   - LinkedIn for professional tools

   - YouTube for tutorials and demos

3. **Content Marketing**
   - Blog posts about your GPT's use cases
   - Tutorial videos showing it in action
   - Case studies from early users

### Launch Strategies

**Soft Launch**
- Share with a small group first
- Collect feedback
- Fix any issues
- Then go wide

**Hard Launch**
- Build anticipation beforehand
- Launch with a promotional offer
- Push across all channels simultaneously
- Create urgency (limited-time pricing)

**Ongoing Promotion**
- Regular content about your GPT
- User testimonials and success stories
- Feature updates and improvements
- Community building
