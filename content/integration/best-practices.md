## 1. Optimize Your GPT Description

Your GPT description is your sales pitch. Make it count.

### Do This:
- **Lead with value** - What problem do you solve?
- **Be specific** - "Save 5 hours per week" beats "Save time"
- **Include use cases** - Help users imagine using it
- **Highlight uniqueness** - What makes you different?

## 2. Set Competitive Pricing

### Research First
- Check what similar GPTs charge
- Consider your target audience's budget
- Use [Authflow's Pricing Optimizer](https://www.authflow.ai/?utm_source=github&utm_medium=docs&utm_campaign=github_docs&utm_content=bestpractices_pricingoptimizer_cta#pricing-optimizer) for suggestions

### Test and Iterate
- Start with your best guess
- Monitor conversion rates
- Adjust based on data
- Consider seasonal promotions

### Price Anchoring
- Show annual savings clearly
- Use the "Save X%" messaging
- Make monthly the "convenient" option

## 3. Offer Free Trials

Free trials dramatically increase conversions.

### Why They Work
- Remove the risk barrier
- Let your GPT prove its value
- Build habit/dependency
- Higher perceived fairness

### Best Practices
- 7 days is the sweet spot for most GPTs
- Require payment info upfront (higher quality leads)
- Send onboarding emails during trial
- Remind users before trial ends

## 4. Create Multiple Tiers

Different users have different needs and budgets.

### Tier Strategy
- **Entry tier** - Affordable, attracts volume
- **Standard tier** - Your main offering, best value
- **Premium tier** - For power users, makes standard look reasonable

### Pricing Psychology
- Make the middle option most attractive
- Use decoy pricing if needed
- Clearly differentiate what each tier includes

## 5. Focus on User Experience

Happy users stay subscribed and refer others.

### First Impression
- Make the verification smooth
- Provide immediate value after signup
- Have clear getting-started guidance

### Ongoing Experience
- Keep your GPT updated
- Fix issues quickly
- Listen to feedback
- Add requested features

### Reduce Churn
- Engage with users regularly
- Provide tips and best practices
- Celebrate their successes
- Offer incentives to stay

## 6. Market Your GPT

Build an audience beyond the ChatGPT Store.

### Content Marketing
- Blog about use cases

- Share success stories
- Write comparison guides

### Social Media
- Twitter/X for AI community

- YouTube for demonstrations
- Reddit (follow community rules!)

### Email Marketing
- Build a list
- Send valuable content
- Announce updates
- Share user wins

### Partnerships
- Collaborate with complementary GPT creators
- Guest post on relevant blogs
- Join AI/GPT communities
- Consider affiliate partnerships

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Pricing too low** - Undervalues your work, harder to raise later
2. **No free trial** - Loses potential customers who need to try first
3. **Poor description** - Doesn't communicate value
4. **Ignoring feedback** - Miss improvement opportunities
5. **Set and forget** - Need ongoing improvement and marketing
6. **No support** - Users feel abandoned
7. **Inconsistent quality** - Damages trust and increases churn
